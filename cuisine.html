Mumbai City Cuisine

Mumbai's cuisine is a delightful reflection of its diverse culture, blending flavors from various communities and regions. Here are some highlights of Mumbai's culinary scene:

Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a popular and flavorful street food dish originating from the streets of Mumbai, India.

Bhel Puri

Bhel Puri is a popular and refreshing Indian street food snack that originated in the state of Maharashtra, particularly Mumbai.

Vada Pav

Vada Pav is a popular and iconic street food snack from the Indian state of Maharashtra, especially associated with the city of Mumbai.
