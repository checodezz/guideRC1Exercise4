Interesting Facts About Mumbai City

Dabbawalas

The Dabbawalas collect freshly prepared lunches from the homes of their clients in the morning. These lunches are typically packed in tiffin boxes or dabbas.

Bollywood

Bollywood produces a diverse range of films, including musicals, dramas, comedies, action films, and more. The industry is known for its colorful and extravagant productions, often featuring elaborate song and dance sequences.

Elephanta Caves

The Elephanta Caves are a UNESCO World Heritage Site located on Elephanta Island in Mumbai Harbor, Maharashtra, India.
