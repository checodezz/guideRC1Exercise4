Top Attractions in Mumbai

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is located at the waterfront in South Mumbai, overlooking the Arabian Sea. It stands at the Apollo Bunder area, near the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel.

Location: Mumbai, India

Chatrapati Shivaji Terminus

Chatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST) is a historic railway station in Mumbai, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its impressive Victorian Gothic architecture and serving as a major transportation hub.

Location: Fort, Mumbai

Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a picturesque promenade in Mumbai, India, known for its "Queen's Necklace" appearance at night, lined with palm trees and facing the Arabian Sea.

Location: South Mumbai Coastal
