Transportation in Mumbai

Local Trains

Local trains in Mumbai are a vital component of the city's public transportation system and play a crucial role in the daily lives of millions of residents.

Best Buses

BEST buses are an integral part of Mumbai's public transportation system and serve as a primary mode of transport for millions of commuters daily.

Metro

Metro systems often include both underground and elevated lines, allowing trains to operate through tunnels beneath the city or on elevated tracks above street level.
